Why Prevalence Rules PPV — the Predictive-Value Curve

Sensitivity and specificity are fixed by the test. But PPV (chance of disease given a positive result) and NPV depend on prevalence. Drag the marker along the curve — and notice PPV lives on the steep, unforgiving part at low prevalence.
