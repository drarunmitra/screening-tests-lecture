Where to Draw the Line — Cut-off, Sensitivity & Specificity

A test gives a number (say a biomarker level). Healthy and diseased people overlap. Wherever you set the cut-off, you trade sensitivity against specificity — you cannot maximise both unless the two groups barely overlap. Drag the line and watch.
