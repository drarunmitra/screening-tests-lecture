Confirm the Positive — Series vs Parallel Testing

1,000 people are screened (each square = one person). A single screen at low prevalence yields mostly false positives. Run a second test and watch what happens — series (confirm the positives) strips out false alarms and lifts PPV; parallel (positive if either) catches more disease but adds false positives.
